Who we are
Built on expertise,
driven by precision

E-HUB Engineering Consultancy — short for Engineering Hub — is a Pokhara-based firm founded by licensed surveyors and engineers committed to delivering accurate, government-standard land and engineering survey services across Nepal.

We combine modern equipment with deep local knowledge of Gandaki Province's terrain, land records, and government processes to serve individuals, developers, and institutions alike.

Our goal is simple: give every client clear, honest, and precise results — on time and within budget.

What we offer
Our core services

Professional survey and engineering services delivered to government and international standards.

📍

Topographic survey

Accurate mapping of natural and man-made terrain features, elevations, and land profiles for engineering and development projects.

📐

Contour mapping

Detailed contour generation and terrain analysis for site planning, drainage design, and earthwork calculations.

📏

Land & cadastral survey

Boundary surveys, land measurements, plot verification, subdivision planning, and cadastral mapping services.

🏗️

Engineering design & planning

Road, site, and infrastructure planning with modern engineering software including Civil 3D and AutoCAD.

📄

DPR & feasibility study

Preparation of Detailed Project Reports (DPR), feasibility studies, technical reports, and project documentation.

✏️

2D drafting & design

Professional CAD drawings, site layouts, floor plans, and engineering drawings for various projects.

🌐

3D modeling & visualization

3D terrain models, corridor modeling, surface analysis, and realistic project visualizations.

🛣️

Road design & Civil 3D services

Road alignment design, profile design, cross-sections, corridor modeling, quantity estimation, and roadway engineering using Civil 3D.

📊

Quantity estimation & BOQ preparation

Preparation of quantity estimates, BOQs, cost estimates, rate analysis, and tender documentation.

🏗️

Construction & construction supervision

Comprehensive construction support including site execution, technical supervision, quality control, progress monitoring, contractor coordination, and project management to ensure successful project completion.

🔍

Construction survey & setting out

Building layout, road centerline setting out, leveling, and construction staking services.

🏘️

Real estate & land services

Complete real estate survey and legal services — all under one roof.

  • Land measurement, plot development & plot sketch preparation
  • Valuation support for banks & courts
  • Map verification & legal documentation assistance
✅

Project management & consultancy

End-to-end project coordination, monitoring, reporting, and technical consultancy from survey to project completion.
